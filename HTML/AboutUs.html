OUR BRANCHES

MERCADINHO

MERCADINHO is a haven for beauty enthusiasts seeking premium cosmetics and skincare essentials. Nestled in the heart of Panadura, this branch is dedicated to curating a selection of top-quality beauty products that cater to every skincare need and beauty preference.

FoodLand

FoodLand, situated in the vibrant neighborhood of Bambalapitiya, is a one-stop destination for all your grocery needs. With a comprehensive range of products spanning from fresh produce to premium cosmetics, this branch caters to the diverse requirements of our valued customers.

Fresh & Easy

Fresh & Easy, nestled in the picturesque town of Kandy, is a haven for discerning shoppers seeking a delightful array of fresh produce and pantry essentials. With a focus on quality and variety, this branch caters to the culinary needs and snacking preferences of our esteemed customers.

The Grocery Store

Nestled in the neighborhood of Maradana, The Grocery Store is a convenient shopping destination offering a diverse selection of products. Providing customers with quality products in a central location, The Grocery Store is your go-to spot for everyday essentials.
